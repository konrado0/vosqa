What is VOSQA?

It is all about questions and answers

  • Anyone in community can vote on answers and questions.
  • Good answers bubble up and are visible first, no need to scroll down to find them.
  • Just questions and answers, zero off topic. If you need to clarify something use comments.

Tags and images help you find interesting questions

  • Author can specify up to 5 tags categorizing questions subject eg. #vosqa or #opensource.
  • Clicking on a tag lets you see all the questions tagged with it.
  • Each question has image associated with it.
  • Quickly find interesting questions by browsing them with our convenient views.

For your activity you earn reputation and unlock badges

  • Reputation is awarded when people vote on your posts.
  • With higher reputation you get more privileges, like ability to vote down posts and comment on them.
  • As you actively participate in community you gain badges. From easy to get bronze ones to gold symbolizing substantial commitment.

QA sites only works when users like you contribute generously advising on other’s problems.
Don’t hesitate and:

  • Find interesting questions
  • Ask a question
